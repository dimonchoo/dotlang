DotLang

Meta-language for human-to-AI communication .[action][.mod] target

Actions

.!Implementcreate, build, add
.?Explainwhat is, how, why
.@Reviewcheck, audit, look at
.#Fixdebug, investigate, repair
.~Refactorsimplify, optimize, clean
.^Plandesign, approach, strategy
.&Analyzeexplore, research, compare
.-Removedelete, drop, clean up
.=Updatechange, modify, rename
.$Gitcommit, push, branch

Modifiers

.aAllentire scope
.qQuickbrief, 2-3 sentences
.dDeepthorough, detailed
.sSelfautonomous, no questions
.oOnlystrict scope, nothing else

> then
